Hello! I’m a Web Developer and UI/UX Designer based in Switzerland. I specialize in creating websites that are not only functional but also engaging and easy to use. My work involves using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks to build responsive and dynamic sites.

I’m passionate about designing user-friendly interfaces that make navigating the digital world seamless. I enjoy the challenge of turning complex problems into simple, beautiful solutions that make a difference in people’s lives.

Outside of work, I enjoy running, reading, and listening to podcasts. These activities keep me inspired and help me stay balanced. Running allows me to clear my mind and find new perspectives, while books and podcasts offer endless opportunities to learn and grow.

Switzerland’s beautiful landscapes provide a perfect backdrop for both work and leisure. I love being part of a community that values innovation and quality, which inspires me to continually improve my skills and stay on top of the latest design trends.

Feel free to browse my portfolio to see examples of my work. If you’re interested in collaborating or just want to chat, don’t hesitate to reach out. I’d love to hear from you!
